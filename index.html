ExChangePlates

ExChangePlates is a community-driven project developed using Java NetBeans, aimed at bridging the gap between surplus food and those in need. Individuals can participate by donating excess food items, and the platform facilitates the seamless collection and distribution of these donations by NGOs to impoverished communities. Through a user-friendly interface, donors can easily register and list available food items for donation, while NGOs can efficiently coordinate pickups and deliveries. This project embodies the spirit of generosity and collaboration, harnessing technology to make a meaningful impact on hunger relief efforts.
